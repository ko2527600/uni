## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- **Device**: Smartphone, tablet, or computer
- **Browser**: Chrome 80+, Firefox 75+, Safari 13+, Edge 80+
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection (3G or better)
- **Storage**: 50MB free space for PWA installation

### Account Registration

#### For Students and Class Representatives
1. Visit the UniPortal website
2. Click "Sign Up" on the login page
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username
   - **Email**: Use your university email if available
   - **Password**: Minimum 6 characters
   - **Full Name**: Your complete name
   - **Role**: Select "Student" or "Class Representative"
   - **Class Code**: Enter the code provided by your Class Rep
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. Check your email for a 6-digit verification code
6. Enter the verification code to activate your account
7. Login with your credentials

#### For Lecturers
1. Follow steps 1-3 above, but select "Lecturer" as your role
2. **No class code required** for lecturers
3. Complete email verification
4. Contact your administrator to claim your courses

### Installing the PWA (Progressive Web App)
1. Open UniPortal in your mobile browser
2. Look for the "Install App" prompt or browser menu option
3. Tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen"
4. The app will be installed on your device like a native app
5. Access UniPortal from your home screen

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Can't log in to my account
**Solutions**:
1. Check your **username and password** are correct
2. Ensure your account is **verified** (check email)
3. Try **password reset** if you've forgotten your password
4. Clear your **browser cache** and cookies
5. Contact your Class Rep or administrator

#### File Upload Issues
**Issue**: Can't upload files or files are rejected
**Solutions**:
1. Check **file format** is supported (PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, PPT, PPTX)
2. Verify **file size** is within limits (varies by subscription)
3. Check your class has **available storage space**
4. Try **renaming the file** to remove special characters
5. Contact your Class Rep about subscription limits

#### Attendance Problems
**Issue**: Can't mark attendance or location not detected
**Solutions**:
1. **Allow location access** when prompted by your browser
2. Ensure you're **physically present** in the classroom
3. Check your **internet connection** is stable
4. Try **refreshing the page** and marking attendance again
5. Contact your lecturer if the session has technical issues

#### Chat and Communication Issues
**Issue**: Messages not sending or receiving
**Solutions**:
1. Check your **internet connection**
2. **Refresh the page** to reconnect
3. Clear **browser cache** and reload
4. Try **logging out and back in**
5. Report persistent issues to your administrator

#### Subscription and Payment Issues
**Issue**: Payment failed or subscription not activated
**Solutions**:
1. **Verify payment details** are correct
2. Check your **bank account** has sufficient funds
3. Try a **different payment method**
4. Contact **Paystack support** for payment issues
5. Reach out to your **Class Rep** for subscription problems
